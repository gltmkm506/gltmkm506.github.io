Partnering with BART in USA's first transit & tea chain collab

With the BART Youth Initiative, made history!

Oct 18, 2024

Alexus Lopez

How it Happened

In a carpool to a personal event, I met the Youth Initiative PM. Knowing and seeing the amazing response from the public at their booths in conventions, the success with mascots overseas, and connecting with the PM, I excitedly discussed collaboration ideas. Bubble tea shops have recently hit a new trend: collaborations with video game publishers and anime IPs. Knowing the market, we thought, why not do something that feels more community-oriented?
