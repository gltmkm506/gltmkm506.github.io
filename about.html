Hi, I'm Lex!

I'm a curious person passionate about creative question-answering. I'm a life-long learner excited to share my knowledge with the world.

Recipient of Brookhaven National Lab's 2022 Quantum and Scientific Computing Fellowship as well as CITRIS EDGE in STEM's 2021 Mentoring Fellowship, I just graduated from UC Berkeley's Division of Computing, Data Science, and Society. This winter, I also completed a Mandarin Chinese program in Taiwan. I'm currently looking for a variety of opportunities in research and computing to explore my passion for Data Science. My interests include interpretable machine learning, data arts & humanities, and NLP.

Working up from publicity to VP, I gave an industry panel at Crunchyroll's first VCRX! On top of being a computer science educator, I also worked in the real estate industry and as an administrator for TDPS at Berkeley.
